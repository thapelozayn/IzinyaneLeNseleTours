About

Izinyane LeNsele Tours is a company that seeks to bring about change in the country by improving domestic tourism. Our main focus is on offering educational tours to all students countrywide with the aim of making them aware of the beauty of Eswatini, getting to know their culture and the importance of travelling. This company was established by the year of 2021, by an International Tourism student of Limkokwing University. It is based at Sidwashini, Mbabane in the Hhohho region. The company aims to promote domestic tourism since the Ministry of Tourism has launched a campaign that promotes domestic tourism during this period of the Novel Corona Virus. The main target of the products and services offered within the company are tertiary students who are willing explore the beauty of Eswatini, workers who also want to refresh and have leisure time and lastly students who are keen to learn about the rich history and culture of Eswatini.

Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every students has access to educational tours that will enable them to know better about the history of Eswatini and the various attractions that the country has more especially for leisure and adventure. To provide financial support on community development as the Corporate Social Responsibility states for a better improvement in community projects.

Vision

To provide quality service to our clients at all times regardless of the challenges that may be encountered and be the leading travel agency in Eswatini that specializes in educational tours and many other tour packages for our clients

Objectives

Ensure the availability of reliable, quality and safe travelling for all tourists in Eswatini and Southern Africa. Offering educational tours that will aspire people to be more responsible travelers and global citizens. Provide travelers with more access and be knowledgeable about the customs of the Kingdom of Eswatini. Ensure that all our travelers see the importance of being eco-friendly and promote domestic tourism for one to be exposed to what the country offers. To make reliable, quality and safe travelling in the next 2 years. To be able to educate people about the importance of sustainable tourism in the 3rd month of our operation. To provide financial assistance to community in the after 3 years of operation as Corporate Social Responsibility. To improve educational tourism in the next 2 years by making it a priority for students to explore and get to know the attractions. To make travellers understand and love the culture of Eswatini by making them to be part of cultural activities every time they travelled to Eswatini. To make local communities to have a good relationship with travellers. To make sure that local and responsible tourism initiatives they are provided with the support they need. To make sure that the community comes first rather meeting demands for tourist. To increase awareness of the history and culture of Eswatini.
