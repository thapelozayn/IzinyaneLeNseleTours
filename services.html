Services

Izinyane LeNsele Tours is a tour operator which creates packages and sell them with no in-between personnel. We aim at creating a bond between the company and the tour destinations as well as the clients. At Izinyane LeNsele Tours we offer educational tours to students at high school and tertiary level as-well as promote domestic tourism within the Kingdom of Eswatini.

Tours

We are a small company that deals with creating educational and leisure tour packages for our potential clients with the aim of promoting domestic tourism, especially in times like this as there is the outbreak of the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19). Our main aim is to organize educational tours for students countrywide. This would play a vital role in motivating people to travel because we as a company strive ourselves in providing all of the information that one needs to travel and also places that could be of interest. The Kingdom of Eswatini is one African country that consists of appealing attractions, most of them which are eco-friendly. Most of these attractions hold the ancient times and the culture of Emaswati. The educational tours will be of benefit to primary and secondary schools as they present the opportunity to students to learn more about their history and also explore the different attractions that the country entails. At tertiary level, students will also benefit from these travel packages as they need a moment to breathe and excuse themselves from their school work as a form of restoration.
